WELCOME TO Viña Los Acantos

In Maule´s Heart, the winery tradition was born.

Since 1895, this winery has stood majestically over the picturesque valley of Loncomilla, in the heart of the Maule Region. The region is characterized by its majestic volcanic mountains and temperate climate that give its wine production its unique style. Viña Los Acantos strives to ensure that each bottle contains a blend of unique nuances in its wines.Based on tradition, the wines of Los Acantos are reflected in the wisdom of its distillers, as well as the pride of the wine culture in the Loncomilla Valley.
